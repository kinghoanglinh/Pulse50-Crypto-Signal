# Pulse50 Use Cases

## Intraday Research Screening

Scan top large-cap crypto assets for short-horizon market structure changes, then rank candidates by probability, confidence, risk tier, and liquidity quality.

## Market Desk Watchlist

Generate a normalized top-asset watchlist every few minutes with data source, freshness, spread, order book imbalance, and risk metadata.

## Signal QA And Calibration

Log predictions and compare them against 5-minute outcomes to evaluate hit rate, Brier score, realized return, and confidence bucket quality.
